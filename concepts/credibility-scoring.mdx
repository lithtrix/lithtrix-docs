---
title: Credibility Scoring
---

Every search result includes a `credibility_score` from 0.5 to 1.0 based on source domain.

| Score | Source |
|-------|--------|
| 1.0 | `.gov` |
| 0.9 | `.edu` |
| 0.8 | BBC, Reuters, AP News, and other major news sources |
| 0.7 | `.org` |
| 0.5 | All other sources |
